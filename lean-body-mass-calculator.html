Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: What is Lean Body Mass (LBM) Composition Calculator?
The Lean Body Mass Calculator is an analytical body composition assessment tool designed to calculate your exact non-fat mass component. By isolating your muscle, bone, and essential organ weight from adipose fat stores, this utility helps users accurately track lean tissue changes and optimize fitness goals.
Q2: How accurate is this calculator?
This system utilizes the clinically validated Boer Formula matrix, which uses anthropometric variables to provide reliable estimates for general tracking purposes. While highly effective for monitoring changes over time, it cannot perfectly reflect unique structural details like real-time variations in bone density or acute water retention, which are best captured through clinical DXA bone scans.
Q3: How do I use it?
Select your physiological gender marker, enter your exact total body weight in kilograms, and specify your current height in centimeters. Click the calculation button to run the formula locally inside your browser client instance.
Q4: Is it free?
Yes. This body composition analysis tool is entirely free, operating without background cloud databases or data-sharing dependencies.
Q5: Who should use it?
This calculator is an indispensable tool for bodybuilders designing specialized hyper-trophy phases, personal trainers setting accurate client protein intake parameters, and individuals seeking to monitor actual muscle growth independently of standard scale weight changes.
