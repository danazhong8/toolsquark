Does this calculator account for leap years?

Yes, the native system JavaScript Date framework inherently normalizes leap year shifts smoothly across all historical metrics.

Why does biological age sometimes differ from chronological age?

Chronological age simply counts calendar intervals. In contrast, biological age measures genuine organic and systemic wear, heavily accelerated by chronic stress profiles, systemic inflammation, and sleep debt.
