Frequently Asked Questions
What clinical rule is utilized here?

This tool integrates Naegele's Rule, the clinical standard that projects gestational milestones by mapping 280 days out from the first day of your last menstrual period (LMP).

Will delivery occur precisely on the predicted date?

Statistically, only about 4% of babies arrive precisely on their estimated due date. Spontaneous onset of labor within a two-week boundary before or after this date is clinically normative.
