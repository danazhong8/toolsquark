Frequently Asked Questions
What is Naegele's Rule?

It is the standard obstetric method estimating delivery by adding 7 days to the LMP and subtracting 3 months.

Will I give birth exactly on this day?

Only about 4% of entries deliver on the exact predicted date. Delivery within two weeks before or after is highly standard.
