How the Due Date Calculator Works

This estimation system applies Mittendorf-Williams and Naegele's rule frameworks. By identifying the entry point of your Last Menstrual Period (LMP) and adjusting for your distinct cycle metrics, it structures an automated timeline map matching typical clinical standards.

Understanding Pregnancy Trimesters

  • First Trimester (Weeks 1 - 12): The milestone foundational phase involving rapid cellular diversification and essential structural architecture mapping.
  • Second Trimester (Weeks 13 - 26): Often referred to as the comfort milestone, where fetal systems refine and external movements begin to manifest.
  • Third Trimester (Weeks 27 - 40): The ultimate growth sprint focusing heavily on mass expansion, respiratory finalization, and birth structural readiness.
