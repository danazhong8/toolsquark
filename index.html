About ToolsQuark

ToolsQuark is a free, privacy-focused platform dedicated to providing accessible, data-driven health and wellness tools. We believe in empowering individuals to take charge of their well-being through clear, instant, and secure self-assessment methods. Our calculators and tests are designed to be intuitive and require no data storage, ensuring your privacy is always protected.
