Features & Commitments

Health Calculator Section

Focuses on the quantitative assessment of physiological indicators, providing tools like BMI, TDEE, Body Fat, Daily Water Intake, and Sleep Cycle calculations. Helps you manage your physical condition through data-driven science.

Mental Health Assessment Section

Based on a scientifically validated psychological assessment system, covering Stress, Anxiety, Burnout, Social Anxiety, and Overthinking tests. Helps you monitor psychological fluctuations and regulate your emotional state.

Lifestyle Tools Section

Dedicated to cultivating positive habits, offering Sleep Optimization, Habit Tracking, and Motivation Score tools. From habit building to goal-driven actions, we help elevate your overall quality of life.

Privacy & Free Commitment

We are committed to being 100% free and registration-free. All calculations and tests are processed locally in your browser in real-time; we do not store or track any personal privacy data, ensuring your assessment process is completely secure.
