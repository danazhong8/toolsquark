Calculators disclose formulas, assumptions, examples, limitations, and references. Original self-checks disclose scoring and validation status. Read the Editorial & Methodology Policy.

Private By Design

Core calculations and assessment answers stay in your browser. Homepage discovery events never include search terms, answers, or calculator inputs. Read the Privacy Policy.
