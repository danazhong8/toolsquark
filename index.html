Frequently Asked Questions

How should I interpret my test results?

Our diagnostics provide a baseline indication of your current state. Results should be viewed as self-awareness indicators rather than clinical diagnoses. If your score falls into a high-risk category, we strongly recommend consulting with a professional healthcare provider.

Is my data safe and private?

Privacy is our top priority. We do not store or track any of your personal health metrics. All calculations and tests are performed locally in your browser.
