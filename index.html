Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI?

BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It is a simple way to measure body fat based on height and weight.

How is BMI calculated?

BMI is calculated by dividing weight by height squared.

What is a healthy BMI?

A healthy BMI is generally between 18.5 and 24.9.
