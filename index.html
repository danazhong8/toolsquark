Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI?

BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It is a straightforward, universally accepted screening tool used to estimate body fat based on an individual's height and weight.

How is BMI calculated?

The mathematical formula divides weight in kilograms by height in meters squared ($BMI = \frac{\text{weight}}{\text{height}^2}$). Our calculator automates this using centimeters for your convenience.

What is a healthy BMI?

A healthy BMI is generally between 18.5 and 24.9, which signifies that your body mass is well-proportioned to your height framework.
