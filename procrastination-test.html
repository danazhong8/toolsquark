Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

💡 How is the Procrastination Index calculated?
This metric uses a standardized 10-item behavioral evaluation matrix based on General Procrastination Scale criteria. Each responsive item scales from 0 to 3 points. The sum cumulative represents non-linear task delay indices, categorizing profiles into standard execution action tiers.
🔒 Is my executive execution or behavioral health data stored?
No. ToolsQuark operates on a strict zero-server footprint standard. All diagnostic state calculations process directly inside your local browser client instance. No trackable analytical cookies, pipeline entries, or behavioral metrics are stored or transmitted.
⚠️ Can this replace a formal clinical behavioral evaluation?
No. This platform is purely for empirical self-screening and situational productivity tracking. It does not issue official psychological signatures or operational disorder verdicts. Please consult a certified professional for dedicated cognitive behavioral therapies.
