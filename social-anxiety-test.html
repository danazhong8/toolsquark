Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

💡 How is the Social Anxiety Index calculated?
This evaluation incorporates an architecture modeled after the Liebowitz Social Anxiety Scale framework. Ten target vectors trace performance comfort thresholds and autonomic avoidance frequency. Each response accumulates a 0-3 loading score, resulting in a 0-30 parameter distribution analysis.
🔒 Is my data safe from monitoring networks?
Yes. ToolsQuark operates on a strict zero-server footprint standard. All diagnostic state calculations process directly inside your local browser client instance. No analytical cookies, profile logging, or identity matching telemetry pipelines are implemented.
⚠️ Can this diagnose clinical social phobias?
No. This index platform measures general situational distress indicators. It represents an educational tool and does not provide valid therapeutic diagnoses. If interaction zones generate severe panic responses, please consult an accredited psychological clinic.
