How to Measure Correctly

For high accuracy, follow strict measurement protocols with a flexible tape measure:

  • Neck: Measure just below the larynx, sloping slightly downward to the front.
  • Waist: Measure horizontally around your natural waistline (for men, at the navel level; for women, at the narrowest point above the navel).
  • Hips (Women Only): Measure around the largest horizontal circumference of the buttocks.

Body Fat Categories (ACE Standards)

According to the American Council on Exercise (ACE), body fat distributions fall into the following structural classifications:

For Men:

Essential Fat: 2-5% | Athletes: 6-13% | Fitness: 14-17% | Acceptable: 18-24% | Obesity: 25%+

For Women:

Essential Fat: 10-13% | Athletes: 14-20% | Fitness: 21-24% | Acceptable: 25-31% | Obesity: 32%+
