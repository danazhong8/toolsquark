FAQ

Is burnout different from everyday stress?
Yes. Stress involves too much pressure (over-engagement), while burnout involves feeling entirely dried out, empty, and unmotivated (disengagement).
Can a long vacation cure deep burnout?
Rarely. Vacations temporarily ease symptoms, but returning to the identical toxic environment or workload brings burnout back quickly.
How can I regain a sense of job efficacy?
Focus on carving out clear boundaries, mastering small internal tasks, and demanding role clarity from your leadership.
