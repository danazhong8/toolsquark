TDEE (TOTAL DAILY ENERGY)

📢 Plain English Summary: Your BMR is the total energy your body burns just keeping you alive at complete rest. Your TDEE represents the real target calories you burn daily including moving, working, and workouts.

💡 Actionable Strategy Tips:
• To lose fat mass safely: Maintain a comfortable macro deficit by consuming around 300-500 kcal below your calculated TDEE.
• To support muscle gain: Aim for a minor calorie surplus coupled with consistent resistance training targets.
