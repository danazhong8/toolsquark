Frequently Asked Questions
How accurate is this converter?

It provides a solid metabolic estimate based on mechanical workload. True burn variations rely heavily on individual walking velocity, metabolic rates, and precise terrain inclines.

Does body weight significantly affect energy expenditure?

Yes. Moving more body mass requires higher kinetic energy and muscular force expenditure. Therefore, individuals with higher body weight inherently burn more calories covering the same distance.
