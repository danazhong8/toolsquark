FAQ

How do I take the measurements accurately?
Measure your neck just below the larynx, keeping the tape flat. Measure your waist horizontally at the narrowest point for females, or precisely at the navel line for males. Ensure the tape is snug but never compressing the skin.
Why does the formula require gender selection?
Biological fat distribution differs fundamentally between genders. Females naturally carry essential reproductive adipose tissue around the hip and pelvic regions, which requires distinct logarithmic constants to analyze accurately.
What is the difference between BMI and Body Fat Percentage?
BMI simply looks at the ratio of your overall weight to your height, completely ignoring what that weight consists of. Body Fat Percentage isolates adipose tissue from muscle, bone, and water, making it a far superior health marker for athletic individuals.
