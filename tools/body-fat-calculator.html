Frequently Asked Questions

What measurements are required?

Men need height, neck, and waist circumference. Women need height, neck, waist, and hip circumference.

How accurate is the U.S. Navy method?

It is a practical field estimate based on circumference measurements. It is useful for tracking trends, but it is not as precise as clinical methods such as DEXA.

Should I use metric or imperial units?

Either works as long as all circumference and height entries use the same unit system selected in the calculator.
