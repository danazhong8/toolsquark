Frequently Asked Questions

Is sleeping 8 hours a strict requirement?

Not necessarily. Sleep efficiency and deep sleep percentages matter more than raw time. Some flourish on 7 hours, while others need 9.

Should I stay in bed if I cannot sleep?

No. If awake after 20 minutes, leave the bed. Do a relaxing, low-light activity and return only when sleepy to avoid associating bed with frustration.

Can blue-light blockers fix my sleep?

They help, but reducing overall cognitive stimulation and dimming house lights 1 hour before bed is vastly more effective.
