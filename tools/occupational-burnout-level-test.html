Frequently Asked Questions

What is the primary indicator of work burnout?

The primary clinical indicator is emotional exhaustion, characterized by feeling deeply drained, chronically tired, and completely depleted of energy before the workday even begins.

How does professional burnout differ from regular fatigue?

Regular fatigue resolves cleanly after a period of restful sleep or a weekend off. Burnout does not improve with standard rest and is typically accompanied by cynicism and a feeling of reduced efficacy.

What is the best immediate recovery action for high burnout?

Establish strict, non-negotiable boundaries around your personal time. Detach completely from professional communications after hours and implement daily micro-rest practices.
