Frequently Asked Questions

Is burnout just being tired?

No. Burnout often combines exhaustion, cynicism or detachment, and reduced professional efficacy.

Can a vacation fix burnout?

Rest can help, but persistent burnout often requires workload, boundary, role, or support changes.

Methodology & Privacy: This self-check uses five four-point items inspired by common burnout dimensions: exhaustion, detachment, and efficacy.

Disclaimer: This is not a clinical diagnosis or workplace assessment. Seek professional support if work strain affects health, safety, or functioning.
