FAQ

Why is WHtR better than BMI?
BMI ignores where fat is stored. WHtR targets central abdominal fat, which is the most dangerous type of fat affecting vital organs.
Where exactly should I measure my waist?
Measure horizontally at the midpoint between your lowest rib and the top of your hip bone, usually right across the belly button line.
