FAQ

Does tea or coffee count towards my intake?
Yes, mild teas and coffees contribute to total fluid volume, but pure water remains the ideal choice for metabolic processes.
What are early signs of dehydration?
Dark yellow urine, a dry mouth, mild headaches, and sudden midday fatigue are classic early warning signals.
