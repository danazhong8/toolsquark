Frequently Asked Questions

Does coffee or tea count toward fluid intake?

Yes, mild caffeinated drinks contribute to total daily fluid intake, though plain water remains the most direct hydration option.

Can I drink too much water?

Yes. Excessive intake can contribute to low blood sodium, especially during long endurance events. Use thirst, urine color, and medical guidance alongside numerical estimates.

Why does exercise increase the result?

Exercise raises sweat and respiratory fluid losses. This calculator adds an activity allowance based on exercise duration.

Methodology & Quality Assurance: This calculator estimates baseline hydration as body weight in kg multiplied by a sex-specific factor, then adds about 0.35 L for every 30 minutes of exercise.

Medical Disclaimer: Hydration needs vary with climate, sodium intake, medical conditions, medications, pregnancy, and sweat rate. This tool is educational and not medical advice.
