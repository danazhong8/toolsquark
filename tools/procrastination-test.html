FAQ

Why do I work better under tight deadline pressure?
The intense fear of immediate failure overrides your emotional avoidance. However, this creates extreme stress and hurts overall quality.
Is procrastination just laziness?
No. Lazy people simply don't care to do the work. Procrastinators desperately want to do the work but are blocked by internal anxiety.
How do I overcome a massive, daunting task?
Micro-segment it. Break the project down until the first individual step feels too ridiculously simple to avoid.
