Frequently Asked Questions
Why do I work better under tight deadline pressure?

The massive surge of stress hormones artificially activates executive planning centers, compensating temporarily for weak intrinsic motivation pathways.

Is procrastination driven primarily by structural laziness?

No. Modern cognitive science demonstrates procrastination is fundamentally an emotional regulation failure—prioritizing immediate mood repair over future long-term welfare.
