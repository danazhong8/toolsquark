Frequently Asked Questions

Is introversion the same as social anxiety?

No. Introverts prefer quiet settings because they recharge alone, but they don't necessarily fear social judgment. Social anxiety is driven by an intense dread of negative evaluation and scrutiny.

Can social anxiety be outgrown naturally?

Mild forms may settle with age as social experience increases, but stubborn social anxiety typically requires intentional, gradual exposure to social triggers to rewire your brain's fear response.

What should I do when experiencing a social panic spike?

Shift your focus outward to break the internal anxious monitoring loop. A proven technique is to describe 3 physical objects in the room or practice controlled deep breathing.

How accurate is this online social anxiety test?

This test is a self-assessment screening tool designed to look for behavioral indicators like evaluation dread and safety behaviors. It is for educational purposes and should not replace a professional clinical diagnosis.

What are common signs of high social anxiety?

Common signs include intense dread before social events, physical symptoms (like rapid heart rate, sweating, or trembling), avoiding eye contact, using safety behaviors (like hiding behind your phone), and long hours of post-event cognitive parsing (overthinking interactions).
