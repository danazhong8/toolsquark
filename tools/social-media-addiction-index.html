Frequently Asked Questions

Why is social media so hard to close down?

Infinite scroll mechanisms eliminate natural stopping cues, making your brain wait indefinitely for the next dopaminergic hit[cite: 15].

Does deleting apps solve the problem?

It provides short-term space, but addressing the underlying emotional void—like boredom or social awkwardness—is vital for permanent change[cite: 15].

Can social media use be purely healthy?

Yes. When used intentionally to schedule offline interactions or learn specific skills, its negative mental impacts drop drastically[cite: 15].
