Frequently Asked Questions
Why is social media so hard to close down?

Infinite scroll mechanisms eliminate natural stopping cues, making your brain wait indefinitely for the next dopaminergic hit.

Does deleting apps solve the problem?

It provides short-term space, but addressing the underlying emotional void—like boredom or social awkwardness—is vital for permanent change.
