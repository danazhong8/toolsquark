FAQ

What is the difference between anxiety and fear?
Fear is a response to an immediate, real threat (like a bear). Anxiety is tension focused on an unpredictable, imagined future threat.
Why does anxiety make me physically exhausted?
Staying in a state of high threat-arousal burns massive amounts of physical energy, leading to deep somatic exhaustion.
Can cutting down caffeine reduce my anxiety spikes?
Yes. Caffeine mimics adrenaline production, which directly triggers hyperarousal in a highly sensitive nervous system.
