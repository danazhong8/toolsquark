Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between problem-solving and overthinking?

Problem-solving focuses actively on generating immediate, actionable pathways to change a situation. Overthinking loops repeatedly around historical mistakes or future catastrophes without actionable conclusions.

How does chronic rumination affect overall health?

Chronic rumination traps the central nervous system in an active fight-or-flight feedback loop, elevating evening cortisol levels, disrupting sleep cycles, and compounding mental fatigue.

What is the most effective immediate cognitive block technique?

Implement the '5-Minute Worry Window' rule. Restrict deliberate planning or analysis to a specific, short block of time daily, writing down thoughts to offload working memory.
