Frequently Asked Questions

Is overthinking always bad?

No. Careful thinking is useful. It becomes a problem when it repeats without progress, rest, or action.

Can this diagnose OCD or anxiety?

No. This is a self-reflection tool and cannot diagnose clinical conditions.

Methodology & Privacy: This self-check uses five four-point items covering rumination, decision paralysis, catastrophizing, and action delay.

Disclaimer: This tool is educational only. Seek professional care if repetitive thoughts are distressing, intrusive, or impairing.
