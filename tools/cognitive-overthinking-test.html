FAQ

Is overthinking the same as being highly analytical?
No. Analysis leads to clear decisions and actions. Overthinking loops continuously without ever reaching a resolution.
Why does my overthinking get worse at night?
Fewer daytime sensory inputs leave your brain alone with its thoughts, while fatigue lowers your prefrontal emotional control.
How do I stop a rumination spiral?
Use the 'worry postponement' technique: schedule a strict 15-minute worry window daily, and defer ruminations outside that period.
