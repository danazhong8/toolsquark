🔍 Frequently Asked Questions

Why does my discipline crash in the evening?

Willpower behaves like a muscle; making decisions all day exhausts your prefrontal cortex, a state known as ego depletion.

Can self-discipline be trained or is it genetic?

While baseline impulsivity has genetic factors, neural plasticity allows systemic volitional control to strengthen over time through habit structuring.
