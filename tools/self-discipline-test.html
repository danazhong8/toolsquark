Frequently Asked Questions
Why does my discipline crash in the evening?

Willpower behaves like a muscle; making decisions all day exhausts your self-regulatory resources, a phenomenon known in cognitive psychology as ego depletion.

Can self-discipline be trained or is it genetic?

While baseline impulsivity has genetic components, volitional control is highly plastic. Developing fixed environmental routines minimizes reliance on raw willpower.
