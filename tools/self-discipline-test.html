Frequently Asked Questions

Why does my discipline crash in the evening?

Willpower behaves like a muscle; making decisions all day exhausts your self-control, making evening temptations harder to resist.

Can discipline kill spontaneity and fun?

No. True discipline buys freedom. Organizing your chores gives you guilt-free time for genuine relaxation.

How do I build self-discipline from scratch?

Start small. Automate one tiny daily habit—like making your bed—before tackling complex lifestyle transformations.
