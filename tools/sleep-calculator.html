Frequently Asked Questions

What is a sleep cycle?

A complete sleep cycle lasts roughly 90 minutes and progresses through light sleep, deep sleep, and REM (dreaming) stages[cite: 13].

Why do I wake up tired after 8 hours?

You might be waking up right in the middle of a deep sleep stage[cite: 13]. Waking up at the end of a 90-minute cycle leaves you feeling significantly fresher[cite: 13].
