FAQ

What is a sleep cycle?
A complete sleep cycle lasts roughly 90 minutes and progresses through light sleep, deep sleep, and REM (dreaming) stages.
Why do I wake up tired after 8 hours?
You might be waking up right in the middle of a deep sleep stage. Waking up at the end of a 90-minute cycle leaves you feeling significantly fresher.
