Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Lean Body Mass (LBM)?

Lean Body Mass represents everything inside your body that is not adipose storage tissue (fat). It encompasses your structural skeletal muscle framework, vital internal organs, dense bone architecture, connective nervous systems, and essential body water. Tracking LBM tells you whether you are gaining true, functional tissue or strictly packing on unneeded fat.

Why is the Boer Formula highly regarded clinically?

The Boer formula is a clinically validated mathematical model designed to isolate active cellular tissue. Unlike generic calculators, it leverages specialized skin and biological coefficients optimized for varying height-to-weight ratios, delivering a non-invasive estimate that correlates strongly with laboratory dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DEXA) scans.

How can I strategically use LBM to fuel lifestyle progress?

Your Lean Body Mass is the primary driver of your resting metabolic rate. Muscle tissue is highly active metabolic machinery; it burns energy continuously even while you rest. Knowing your true LBM allows you to establish hyper-accurate, custom daily caloric targets, preventing severe calorie deficits from catabolizing your hard-earned muscle tissue during fat loss phases.
