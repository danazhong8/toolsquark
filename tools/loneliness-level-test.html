FAQ

Is feeling lonely the same as being alone?
No. Being alone is a physical state of solitude, which can be peaceful and restoring. Loneliness is the distressing feeling of being disconnected or misunderstood by others.
Can this assessment indicate a depressive disorder?
No. This tool measures temporary emotional and social isolation scores. It is strictly for self-reflection and personal growth monitoring.
How can I improve my connectedness score?
Try initiating small, quality conversations, joining local hobby clubs, reducing passive scrolling, and prioritizing deep face-to-face interactions.
