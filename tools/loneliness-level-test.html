Frequently Asked Questions

Can I be lonely even with friends?

Yes. Loneliness is about perceived connection quality, not just the number of people around you.

Is loneliness a mental illness?

No, but chronic loneliness can affect mood, stress, sleep, and health, and support can help.

Methodology & Privacy: This self-check uses five four-point items covering perceived support, isolation, belonging, and reach-out barriers.

Disclaimer: This is an educational self-reflection tool and not a clinical assessment. Seek professional or community support if loneliness feels persistent or unsafe.
