FAQ

Is high screen time automatically an addiction?
No. If your screen use is purely for active work or creation, it differs from passive, compulsive scrolling driven by boredom or escape.
Why do I feel anxious when my phone battery is low?
This is known as 'Nomophobia'. It triggers a survival-like anxiety rooted in the fear of being digitally disconnected or isolated.
Will a digital detox fix my habits permanently?
Temporary detoxes provide clarity, but long-term success relies on setting rigid digital boundaries, like screen-free bedrooms.
