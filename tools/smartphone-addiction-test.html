Frequently Asked Questions

Is frequent phone use always addiction?

No. The concern is loss of control, distress, and interference with sleep, work, study, or relationships.

What is one practical first step?

Turn off nonessential notifications and keep the phone away from bed.

Methodology & Privacy: This self-check uses five four-point items covering checking, dependency, scrolling control, and life disruption.
