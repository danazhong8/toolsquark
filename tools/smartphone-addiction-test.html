Frequently Asked Questions
Is high screen time automatically an addiction?

No. If your screen volume is driven by deliberate, high-value professional output or structured learning, it represents functional utility rather than behavioral addiction.

What is the fastest way to curb phone addiction?

Moving your device to a grayscale display profile and enforcing physical boundary limits—such as keeping the charger outside the bedroom—yields immediate behavioral containment.
