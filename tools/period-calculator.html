Frequently Asked Questions

When does peak ovulation typically occur?

Biological ovulation reliably takes place exactly 14 days before your next menstrual cycle phase begins. However, stress or sleep changes can shift this day.

How can acute lifestyle stress delay a calendar period?

High cortisol surges directly suppress upstream hypothalamic signaling, temporarily delaying regular hormonal cycles and affecting your ovulation timeline.

What is considered a normal menstrual cycle length?

A standard medical menstrual cycle typically ranges between 21 to 35 days for healthy adults, with a bleeding duration lasting between 2 to 7 days.
