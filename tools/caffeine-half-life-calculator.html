How This Calculator Works

The calculator uses an exponential half-life model: remaining caffeine equals dose multiplied by 0.5 raised to elapsed hours divided by selected half-life. It is useful for planning because caffeine does not disappear all at once.

Limits

Actual caffeine clearance varies with genetics, pregnancy, medications, liver function, nicotine use, and individual sensitivity. This page is educational and does not diagnose insomnia or caffeine sensitivity.
