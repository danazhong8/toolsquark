Frequently Asked Questions
Is 10,000 steps a hard rule?

No, it originated as a marketing campaign. Health benefits, such as cardiovascular improvement and metabolic regulation, are observed starting from 7,500 to 8,500 daily steps.

How should I progress my step goals?

If your baseline is low, increase your daily target by 1,000 steps each week to avoid overuse injuries and ensure metabolic sustainability.
