Frequently Asked Questions

How fast should I increase daily steps?

A gradual increase is usually easier to sustain and may reduce joint stress. Many people do best by adding volume over several weeks rather than jumping immediately.

Is 10,000 steps required for health?

No single number fits everyone. Benefits can begin below 10,000 steps, especially when moving from a low baseline. The best target is one you can build toward consistently.

What if my baseline is already above the goal?

If your current baseline is above the selected goal, the plan will keep you near your current level rather than reducing your target.
