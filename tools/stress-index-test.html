Frequently Asked Questions

Can I experience high stress without feeling anxious?

Yes. Stress often manifests physically first—via jaw clenching, tight shoulders, digestive issues, or chronic low energy.

What is the fastest way to drop my stress index?

Engage the parasympathetic nervous system directly via deep, extended-exhalation breathing or taking a brief walk in nature.

Is all stress caused by external work pressures?

No. Internal expectations, poor nutrition, lack of sleep, and unexpressed emotions generate massive internal stress loads.
