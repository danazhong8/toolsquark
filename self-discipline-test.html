Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

💡 How is the Self-Discipline Index calculated?
This index targets advanced executive control frameworks, measuring impulse inhibition levels and long-range goal adherence. Ten specific behavioral friction parameters weight from 0 (optimal internal control) to 3 (systemic choice exhaustion), presenting a baseline metric map of cognitive discipline consistency.
🔒 Are my personal habit parameters shared anywhere?
No. ToolsQuark operates on a strict zero-server footprint standard. All diagnostic state calculations process directly inside your local browser client instance. No performance profiles, habit databases, or telemetry logs are initialized or transmitted.
⚠️ Does a low score signify an executive cognitive disorder?
No. Discipline indices vary based on environmental friction, immediate fatigue loads, and intrinsic reward structures. This matrix is educational and holds no neural diagnostic standing. For clinical evaluation of chronic focus anomalies, consult an executive neurological professional.
