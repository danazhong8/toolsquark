Evidence-Based Design, Utmost Confidentiality

The psychological indicators provided on ToolsQuark are modeled after globally recognized self-report scale structures—such as industry parameters for work-related exhaustion scoring, somatic anxiety inventories, and peer-reviewed cognitive tension scales. We aim to translate psychometric frameworks into elegant, understandable milestones. Because we process data exclusively via front-end client side script, none of your vulnerable mood logs or inputs can ever be intercepted or stored.

Disclaimer: ToolsQuark offers self-awareness screening instruments designed purely for informational and lifestyle monitoring purposes. These instruments do not substitute for clinical mental health therapy, professional medical diagnoses, psychiatry, or professional psychological intervention. If you are experiencing prolonged acute distress, please contact a certified mental health counselor immediately.
