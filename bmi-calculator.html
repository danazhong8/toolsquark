Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a universal parameters metric utilized to scale body fat approximations contextually derived through height/weight metrics.

How is BMI calculated?

The calculation divides an individual's mass in kilograms by their height in meters squared: Formula = (Weight in kg) / (Height in meters)².
