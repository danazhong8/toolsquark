Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

💡 How is the Anxiety Index calculated?
This analytical instrument maps somatic nervous system hyperarousal loops using frameworks calibrated with Generalized Anxiety Disorder metrics. Across 10 clinical items evaluating tension parameters, scores are recorded from 0 to 3 points per node, rendering a definitive index map bounded from 0 to 30.
🔒 Are my internal tension scores protected from external extraction?
Yes. ToolsQuark operates on a strict zero-server footprint standard. All diagnostic state calculations process directly inside your local browser client instance. No database records, tracking identifiers, or hyperarousal logs are transferred or aggregated.
⚠️ Can an intense anxiety index score substitute an official psychotherapeutic signature?
No. High score parameters indicate systemic tension loops but do not equate to a psychiatric diagnostic signature. This engine serves purely as an educational tracker for general mental wellness boundaries. For accurate clinical screening, seek out a credentialed clinical psychotherapist.
