Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is this tool?

This is a digital screening tool designed to help users identify patterns of generalized hyperarousal and persistent somatic nervous tension.

Q2: How accurate is this calculator?

This tool serves as an educational screener for self-reflection. It is not a clinical diagnostic instrument.

Q3: How do I use it?

Answer the 10 brief questions, and the engine will instantly generate a summary of your results.

Q4: Is it free?

Yes, this assessment tool is completely free for all users.

Q5: Who should use it?

It is intended for individuals who want to better understand their stress responses or patterns of chronic worry.
