Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is this tool?
This is a digital screening tool designed to help users identify patterns of generalized hyperarousal and persistent somatic nervous tension.
Q2: How accurate is this calculator?
This tool serves as an educational screener for self-reflection. It is not a clinical diagnostic instrument and should not replace professional medical or psychological evaluation.
Q3: How do I use it?
Simply answer the 10 brief questions about your current cognitive and somatic state, and the engine will instantly generate a summary of your results.
Q4: Is it free?
Yes, this assessment tool is completely free for all users.
Q5: Who should use it?
It is intended for individuals who want to better understand their stress responses, feelings of hyper-vigilance, or patterns of chronic worry.
