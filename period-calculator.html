How the Period Tracker Works

This utility computes biological forecasts using calendar-based calculation frameworks. By mapping the entry values against standard calendar metrics, it tracks patterns to deliver safe estimates for future physiological cycles.

Understanding Your Cycle Metrics

  • Menstrual Cycle: Measured from the exact first day of one menstrual phase up until the first day of the following arrival phase. Standard windows average 28 days.
  • The Ovulation Window: Typically happens 14 days prior to your next upcoming period cycle. Identifying this allows individuals to map their natural family planning protocols accurately.
  • The Fertile Window: Includes the specific ovulation calendar milestone alongside the preceding 5 days, tracking the survival lifespan of reproductive cells.
