FAQ

Does a low focus score mean I have ADHD?
Not necessarily. Modern lifestyle factors like sleep deprivation, digital notification overload, and chronic stress mimic focus deficits closely.
How long can an average adult maintain deep focus?
Typically 45 to 90 minutes. Trying to push past this without strategic cognitive rest results in a steep decline in efficiency.
What is the best way to train my focus?
Practice monotasking. Start with short, dedicated blocks (like the Pomodoro technique) and gradually extend your deep work duration.
