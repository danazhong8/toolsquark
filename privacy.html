Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

ToolsQuark provides browser-based calculators and educational self-checks. This policy explains what stays in your browser, what ordinary hosting systems receive, and how external links are handled.

1. Tool Inputs And Results

Calculator inputs, assessment answers, scores, and result dimensions are processed by JavaScript in your browser. They are not included in page URLs, and ToolsQuark does not send them to an application database or analytics service. Assessment answers reset when you reload or leave the page.

2. Hosting And Security Data

Like any website, our hosting and security providers receive ordinary request information needed to deliver and protect the site. This can include the requested page, timestamp, IP address, browser user agent, and network or security signals. This operational data does not include the answers or scores you enter into a tool.

3. Cookies And Analytics

ToolsQuark currently does not load advertising cookies or third-party analytics scripts. Assessment pages expose browser-only event hooks for quality testing, but those events contain no answers, scores, or result dimensions and are not transmitted unless an analytics service is enabled in the future. We will update this policy before activating a new analytics service.

4. Clipboard And External Links

A result is copied to your clipboard only when you choose the Copy summary command. ToolsQuark pages also link to external organizations and resources. Their privacy practices apply after you follow those links.

5. Your Choices

  • You can leave or reload a tool page to clear the current answers.
  • You can avoid the Copy summary command if you do not want a result placed on your device clipboard.
  • You can use browser controls to clear site data and restrict cookies.

6. Policy Updates

Material changes will be published on this page with a revised update date.
